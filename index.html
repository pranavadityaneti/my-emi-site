FAQs

Got questions? Here are the answers.

Everything you need to know about getting started with Upplix.

Is Upplix free to download?

Yes! Upplix is completely free to download. You can start tracking up to 5 habits right away with no credit card required.

How do I track my habits in Upplix?

Simply create a habit, set your daily goal, and check in each day. Upplix tracks your streaks automatically and sends reminders so you never miss a day.

Does Upplix work offline?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device and syncs to the cloud automatically as soon as you're back online.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes up to 5 habits, daily check-ins, streak tracking, and a weekly progress summary — everything you need to get started.

Can I use Upplix with my team or family?

Absolutely. The Scale plan includes shared workspaces and multi-user access, perfect for teams and families building habits together.
